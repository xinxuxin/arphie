Iced coconut latte recipe:

Use 2 shots of espresso, about 2 ounces total. Add 4 ounces of cold whole milk and 2 ounces of coconut milk. Pour over ice and stir with 1 teaspoon of maple syrup if a sweeter drink is desired.

Hot coconut latte recipe:

Use 2 shots of espresso, about 2 ounces total. Steam 5 ounces of whole milk with 2 ounces of coconut milk until warm and lightly foamy. Pour the milk mixture over the espresso and finish with a small pinch of cinnamon.

Ingredients:

The ingredients used in the coconut latte are espresso, whole milk, coconut milk, ice for the iced version, optional maple syrup, and optional cinnamon.
